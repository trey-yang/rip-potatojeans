ABOUT ME

I am an undergraduate student at Boston University studying Astronomy and Physics, with a minor in Computer Science. I am a member of the BU Astronomical Society executive board.

My hometown is a small city in the south-western mountains of China, called Guiyang. My city, and this part of China in general, is famous for spicy food which I miss a whole lot.

I play the saxophone pretty well. Right now I'm also trying to learn guitar.

I am a licensed ham radio operator (call sign KC1GFQ). Currently my "rig" is just 2 cheap handhelds which I used to volunteer in the Boston Marathons.
