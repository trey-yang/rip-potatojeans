ABOUT ME

I graduated from Boston University with a B.A. in Astronomy and Physics. I am starting in the Master's program in Astronomy and Astrophysics at Australian National University in 2023.

My hometown is a small city in the south-western mountains of China, called Guiyang. Ever since I came back in 2020 due to COVID-19 I have been exploring and re-discovering this part of China, particularly the surrounding nature and spicy food - often accompanied by many of the wonderful people here.

I play the saxophone pretty well. Right now I'm saving up for an EWI, while also trying to learn the guitar as well as electronic music production. I hope you'll be able to listen to my creations one day.

I am a licensed ham radio operator (call sign KC1GFQ) in the United States. I am interested in radio telemetry, APRS, and emergency communications.

I am also starting to offer bespoke website designs to artists and creators I like, so if I told you about it and asked you to check out this website, well, that'd be why :)
