ABOUT ME

I graduated from Boston University with a B.A. in Astronomy and Physics. I am currently applying to graduate programs all over the world.

My hometown is a small city in the south-western mountains of China, called Guiyang. My city, and this part of China in general, is famous for spicy food and I do miss that a whole lot.

I play the saxophone pretty well. Right now I'm also trying to learn guitar.

I am a licensed ham radio operator (call sign KC1GFQ). Currently my "rig" is just 2 cheap handhelds which I use to volunteer in the Boston Marathons.
